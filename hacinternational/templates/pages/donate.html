We are a charity and rely on donations. These donations are a vital source of income and as such they are welcome all the time. Below are secure ways you can donate.

How else can I help?

Become a Volunteer

From time to time we have volunteers who host small events in order to raise funds for the charity. We welcome all the aid we can get and if you can organise events in aid of our charity, we will always welcome this.

You can check our latest volunteering opportunities here.

Donate things you no longer use

Poor communities can use most things we take for granted: Old clothes, appliances, mobile phones bicycles and much more. We package all these donations and send them away for redistribution. As such we welcome everything deemed useful.

Get in touch and we will make sure your donations will get to the people in need.

Attend our events

As we hold a number of events annually. you can help by becoming a member of our community who attends our events. By supporting us on these events we enhance our fundraising ability.

Check our latest events.
